Spidy's controller screen

Designing a complete computer based hexapod is outside the skillset of most developers. You must deal with several components. That was the case for the Tech Museum. I have such a broad range of experience, it was not difficult. I based it off a Raspberry Pi 2B and required setup of several products (e.g. wifi access point). Programs written in HTML, javascript, C++, python 3 and PHP.
